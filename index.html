Our Programs

Bible Classes

Join our free Bible classes to learn and grow spiritually. We offer classes for all ages and skill levels, from beginners to advanced. Whether you're new to the faith or want to deepen your understanding, our Bible classes are open to everyone.

Sports

Get active with our sports programs. We offer various activities for all ages, including soccer, basketball, and volleyball. Join us to stay fit, make new friends, and enjoy the thrill of competition!

Voluntary Work

Make a difference by volunteering with us. Our voluntary work program gives you the opportunity to help others in need, whether through community outreach, charity events, or local initiatives.

Music

Our music program offers lessons in various instruments and vocal training. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced musician, there's a place for you in our community of music lovers.
